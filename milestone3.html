Milestone 3: Success

Outboard motors, invented in 1909, are portable propulsion devices mounted on the stern of small boats. They consist of an engine, gearbox, and propeller, providing maneuverability and propulsion. Their compact size and versatility make them ideal for various watercraft, from fishing boats to recreational vessels. Outboard motors have significantly enhanced the ease of navigation and accessibility for boaters worldwide, becoming a staple in maritime transportation and leisure activities.
